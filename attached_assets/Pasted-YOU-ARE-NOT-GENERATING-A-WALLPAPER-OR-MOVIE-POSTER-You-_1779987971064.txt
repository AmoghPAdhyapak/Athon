YOU ARE NOT GENERATING A WALLPAPER OR MOVIE POSTER.

You are generating a HIGH CTR YouTube gaming thumbnail optimized for clicks, emotional impact, and mobile visibility.

THUMBNAIL GOALS:

* Maximize click-through-rate (CTR)
* Strong emotional impact
* High readability on mobile
* Clear visual storytelling
* Cinematic but not cluttered
* Fast visual understanding in under 1 second

THUMBNAIL COMPOSITION RULES:

* Main character must dominate the frame (45-60% of image)
* Character face must be clearly visible
* Strong facial emotion required
* Add cinematic depth:

  * foreground
  * midground
  * background
* Use strong subject-background separation
* Use dramatic lighting and contrast
* Avoid empty dead space
* Avoid flat poster-like symmetry
* Add action storytelling elements
* Ensure text-safe zones remain visible
* Thumbnail should feel intense and dangerous

VISUAL STORYTELLING RULES:

* Add signs of combat and chaos
* Include enemies, danger, or destruction
* Use motion blur where needed
* Add sparks, smoke, fire particles, aura energy, explosions, or environmental effects
* Make the scene feel alive and in motion
* Show tension and survival pressure

CTR PSYCHOLOGY RULES:

* Create curiosity
* Create emotional tension
* Make viewers ask:
  “WHAT HAPPENED HERE?”
* Focus on impossible or high-stakes moments
* Prioritize emotion over beauty
* Thumbnail should instantly communicate:
  dominance, danger, revenge, survival, rage, or clutch moment

TEXT RULES:

* Text must be BIG and readable on mobile
* Use short aggressive wording
* Avoid generic text like:
  “EPIC GAMEPLAY”
  “BEST MATCH”
* Prefer emotional/high-CTR wording like:
  “1 HP DEMON”
  “THEY PUSHED THE WRONG PLAYER”
  “THIS SHOULD BE IMPOSSIBLE”
  “ONE SHOT. FOUR BODIES.”

LIGHTING RULES:

* Use cinematic high-contrast lighting
* Add glow around main subject
* Prioritize face and weapon visibility
* Use strong rim lighting
* Use fire, neon, or cinematic highlights
* Maintain dark dramatic atmosphere

SCENE ANALYSIS PRIORITY:
Analyze:

* main subject
* weapon/action
* emotional focus
* strongest visual object
* environment intensity
* possible storytelling opportunities

Then optimize the thumbnail around those elements.
